About Us
Spice Tadka aims to bring high-quality spices, herbs, and seasoning to the Indian food Industry. Spice Tadka brings some of the best natural and chemical-free spices grown organically in our farms located in Sirsi, Karnataka. We aim to deliver homegrown spices along with some of the finest spices sourced from farmers who grow the product organically. Spice Tadka not only brings the finest grown products but also helps the farmers to bring you the finest quality of products. Spice Tadka encourages and supports woman's participation in the workforce.
